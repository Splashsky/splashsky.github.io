What is this?

This is a tool for the ADT call center teams to format various customer information specifically for internal processes. It simply helps things stay organized and consistent!

How do I use it?

Simply fill out the form to the left and click the Copy button below! If it was successful, the button will say "Copied!" and you'll be able to paste the result wherever you need to. Click the clear button to clear the form. Click the links above to change the format!

Do you store anything?

Not at all. There's no information being stored. There's not even any cookies on this page. All the stuff you input simply disappears. If you want to check the code for this program, click here!
