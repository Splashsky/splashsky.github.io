ADT Assist Chat Format

What is this?

This is a tool for the ADT Account Management team to format their questions specifically for the internal assist chat. It simply helps things keep organized and consistent!

How do I use it?

Simply fill out the form to the left and click the Copy button below! If it was successful, the button will say 'Copied!" and you'll be able to paste the result into Skype.
